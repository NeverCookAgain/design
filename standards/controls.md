# Controls
## Movement
The game should use standard WASD keyboard character movement, with W being to walk forward, A being walking left, S being walking backward, and D being walking right. In this case, turning the characters head, or moving the camera side-to-side & up-down by moving around the computer's mouse would allow the player to look around. 

## Jumping
> [!NOTE]
> There is currently no jumping feature; this is just thinking about the future.

If jumping were to be a feature implemented, the space bar would be an option for that. 

## Opening containers
To open cupboards, drawers, or anything that holds a "special item", we could use the left button on the mouse. 

## Picking up items
To pick up items, you could use the E or F key (TBD) as either options are close enough to WASD to be comfortable to use.

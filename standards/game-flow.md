# Game flow
## Title screen
After booting the game, the player will enter the title screen. It should be transparent with only the game's logo showing and a button that moves them to the next section.

## Players get orders from customers
Each player starts at the front of the restaurant to receive the order of the customers. Each order is unique and has specific ingredients and five random [special requests](/standards/special-requests/README.md).
